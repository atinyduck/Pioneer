World 1: First Steps

Welcome to the tutorial.
This level is here to teach you the basics of Python and movement in the game.
Use your code to guide the drone from the start to the green goal.
You can choose either route from the corner, so there is no single exact solution.
Take your time and focus on learning how the drone moves when your code runs.

This is a tutorial level, so try simple movement commands and watch what happens.

Available Commands
move() - move the drone forward by 1 space.
turn(x) - turn the drone by x degrees.
pickup() - pick up the box in front of the drone.
drop() - drop the box in the square in front of the drone.
