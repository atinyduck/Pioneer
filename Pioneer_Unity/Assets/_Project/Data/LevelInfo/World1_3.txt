World 1: Level 3

Welcome to the next tutorial level.
This time you will learn how to use boxes and buttons to open doors.
Your goal is to move the box onto the button so the door opens, then guide the drone safely to the green goal.

This puzzle is still about learning, so take your time and watch how the box changes the level when it is placed correctly.
You may need to move, turn, and interact with the box to solve it.

Try picking up the box and dropping it on the button to open the door.

Available Commands
move() - move the drone forward by 1 space.
turn(x) - turn the drone by x degrees.
pickup() - pick up the box in front of the drone.
drop() - drop the box in the square in front of the drone.
